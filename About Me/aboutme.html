Chemical Engineering

I find that being able to create anything with just elements extremely exciting. I believe that a deep understanding of elements will allow us to create innovative, productive, and durable products. I also find the concept of recycling to be very intriguing. The thought of taking the elements from an object that is either broken or useless and turning those elements into a product that is extremely useful is fascinating to me.

Personal Interests

I am interested in the animal biology field. Specifically, the psychology of different species of animals. I am also interested in the field of forensics science. I participated in HOSA, a biology contest and competed as part of the forensics team. There, I learned many ways to identify people from the evidence and how to recreate crime scenes. However, I found entomology in forensics extremely compelling.

Aspirations

In a world of finite materials, we have to conserve as much as possible. This would mean that we would have to make the most of the materials that we create. Through my works as a chemical engineer, I want to create more effective and durable products. As a result, there would be less waste which will lead to less materials being burned and wasted.
